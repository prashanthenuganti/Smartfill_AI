Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08-JULY-2026

Mitra Fill ("we," "us," "our") provides an AI-powered tool that extracts data from identity and education documents (such as Aadhaar, PAN, and educational certificates) to automatically fill Indian government exam and application forms, via a web application and a Chrome browser extension.

What information we collect

Documents you upload
Photos or scans of identity/education documents (e.g., Aadhaar, PAN, SSC/Inter/Degree certificates) that you choose to upload for the purpose of extracting form-fill data.

Extracted personal information
Data our AI extracts from your uploaded documents, which may include: name, date of birth, gender, address, Aadhaar number, PAN number, educational details, and similar identity/education information printed on the documents you upload.

Manually entered information
Mobile number and email address, if you choose to enter them directly.

Account information
If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google to identify your account.

Usage information
Basic technical logs (e.g., timestamps of login/logout, which document types were processed) used for security and troubleshooting.

How we use this information

  • To extract data from your uploaded documents
  • To let you review, correct, and confirm extracted data before use
  • To fill government exam/application forms on your behalf via the Chrome extension, only on pages you actively choose to fill
  • To operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service

We do not use your personal information for advertising, and we do not sell your data.

How your documents and data are processed

Document extraction (Anthropic Claude API)
To extract data from your uploaded documents, images are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Because this uses Anthropic's commercial API (not their free consumer product), your document data is never used to train Anthropic's AI models. Anthropic automatically deletes this data from their systems within 7 days, retaining it only briefly for abuse-monitoring purposes. More information: anthropic.com/legal/commercial-terms

Document retention on our systems
Uploaded document images are used to extract data and are not permanently stored — they are deleted when your session ends.

Extracted profile data
The data extracted from your documents (name, DOB, etc.) is held temporarily in memory during your active session, tied to your logged-in account, so it can be reviewed and used to fill a form. It is not shared with other users and is cleared when your session ends or the server restarts.

Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic (Claude API), to extract data from your documents, as described above
  • Google, for sign-in authentication
  • Law enforcement or regulators, only if legally required

Data security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls limiting who can view extracted data.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us below. You may also stop using the extension and revoke its permissions at any time via Chrome's extension settings.

Children's privacy

Mitra Fill is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
